Chocolate milkshake

Ingredients

  1. 3/4 cup milk
  2. 1/4 cup chocolate-flavored syrup
  3. 3 scoops (1/2 cup each) vanilla ice cream

Instructions

  1. In blender, place milk and syrup. Cover and blend on high speed 2 seconds.


  2. Add ice cream. Cover and blend on low speed about 5 seconds or until smooth. Pour into glasses. Serve immediately.


  3. Serve with whipped cream, chocolate sauce and a cherry.
