Fruit Salad

Ingredients

  1. 1/4 cup honey
  2. 1/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  3. zest of 1 lemon

Salad

  1. 1 lb strawberries, hulled and quartered
  2. 6 oz blueberries
  3. 6 oz raspberries
  4. 3 kiwis, peeled and sliced
  5. 1 orange, peeled and wedges cut in half
  6. 2 apples, peeled and chopped
  7. 1 mango, peeled and chopped
  8. 2 cups grapes

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl whisk together honey, orange juice, and lemon zest.


  2. Add fruit to a large bowl and pour over dressing, tossing gently to combine.


  3. In a separate bowl mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder. Set aside.


  4. Chill until ready to serve.
